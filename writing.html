sportswriting

Aside from being a UX design student, I am a student journalist. I began writing for the Michigan Daily freshman year covering various small sports. I moved on to become a softball beat writer my sophomore year and a hockey beat writer the following year.

My experience on the Daily has made me a more concise writer as well as taken me to some pretty cool places. My favorite memory from being a sportswriter came in January 2019 when I traveled to New York City to cover a Michigan hockey game at Madison Square Garden.
